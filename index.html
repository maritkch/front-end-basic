Members of the Rammstein

Richard Kruspe

(born 24 June 1967 in Wittenberge as Sven Kruspe) is lead guitar player and background singer for Rammstein

Oliver Riedel

(born 11 April 1971) is the bassist for the band Rammstein

Christoph Schneider

(born 11 May 1966, East Berlin) is the drummer for the band Rammstein

Till Lindemann

(born 4 January 1963) is a German musician, qualified pyrotechnician, actor, and poet. He is the lead vocalist for both Rammstein

Paul Landers

(born as Heiko Paul Hiersche, 9 December 1964, East Berlin) is the rhythm guitarist for the band Rammstein

Christian Lorenz

(born 16 November 1966, East Berlin) is the keyboardist for the band Rammstein
